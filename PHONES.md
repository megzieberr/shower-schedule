## 🍏 iPhone / iPad

**Important:** notifications will **not work at all** until you add the app to
your home screen and open it from there. Please don't skip steps 1–4.

1. Open the link above in **Safari** (the blue compass app). Other browsers won't work for this.
2. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom — the square with an arrow pointing up ⬆️.
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**, then tap **Add** (top right).
4. Close Safari. Find the new **Shower Schedule** icon on your home screen and tap it to open.
5. Type your **first name** and tap **Add me**.
6. When it asks about notifications, tap **Allow**.

✅ **Test:** you should get a little test notification. If you do, you're all set!

---

## 🤖 Android

1. Open the link above in **Chrome**.
2. If a box pops up saying **Install app**, tap it. (If not: tap the **⋮** menu in
   the top right and tap **Add to Home screen** / **Install app**.)
3. Open the new **Shower Schedule** icon from your home screen.
4. Type your **first name** and tap **Add me**.
5. When it asks about notifications, tap **Allow**.

✅ **Test:** you should get a little test notification. If you do, you're all set!

---

## How to use it (everyone)

- You'll see times from **17:00 to 23:00**. Tap an open time to book your shower.
- You get **one shower a day**. Tapping a new time just **moves** you there.
- To cancel, tap your own slot and tap **Cancel my shower**.
- Each evening at **17:00** you'll get a reminder to pick your time, and you'll get
  a "shower now" ping when your booked time arrives.

---

## Oops — I tapped "Block" / "Don't Allow" for notifications

No problem, you can turn it back on:

**iPhone:** open the phone's **Settings** app → scroll to **Shower Schedule** →
tap **Notifications** → turn **Allow Notifications** on. Then open the app and try
the menu (⚙️) → **Turn on notifications**.

**Android:** open **Settings** → **Apps** → **Shower Schedule** →
**Notifications** → turn them on. Then open the app, tap the menu (⚙️) →
**Turn on notifications**.
